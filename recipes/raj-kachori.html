|| Raj-Kachori ||

Description :

Raj kachori is an Indian chaat made with a large puri, filled with kabuli chana, potato, sprouts, sweetened curd along with other chaat components like sev, green chutney, sweet chutney. Raj kachori is one of my favourite chaat and order wherever I find it in the menu in restaurants.

  • Street Food (chaat)
  • North-Indian
  • Prep Time 10 minutes
  • Cook Time 30 minutes
  • Servings 10

Ingredients :

    For Kachori:
  • 1½ cup maida / plain flour / refined flour
  • ½ cup rava / semolina / suji, fine
  • ¼ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp hot oil
  • ½ cup water, to knead
  • oil for frying
    • For Chaat:
  • 1 potato, boiled & peeled
  • ½ cup chickpea / chana, soaked & boiled
  • ½ cup green moong dal, soaked & boiled
  • 1 cup curd
  • ¼ cup green chutney
  • ¼ cup tamarind chutney
  • 1 tsp kashmiri chilli powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp chaat masala
  • 1 tsp aamchur / dry mango powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ cup sev
  • ¼ cup boondi
  • ½ cup pomegranate
  • 2 tbsp coriander, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp onion, finely chopped

Steps :

  1. firstly take a poori and make a hole. poori turns crisp once cooled completely (after 2 hours)
  2. stuff with 1 tbsp boiled potato, 1 tbsp boiled chana and 2 tbsp boiled moong dal.
  3. also pour 2 tbsp curd.
  4. now pour a tsp of green chutney and tamarind chutney.
  5. also sprinkle chilli powder, cumin powder, chaat masala, aamchur and salt.
  6. further drizzle half tsp of curd, green chutney and tamarind chutney.
  7. garnish with 2 tbsp sev, 1 tbsp boondi, 1 tbsp pomegranate, 1 tsp coriander and 2 tsp onion.
  8. finally, enjoy raj kachori immediately as a chaat.
